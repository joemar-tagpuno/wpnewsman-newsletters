Getting Started


Customize Template:

Customize your template by clicking on the style editor tabs up above. Set your fonts, colors, and styles.

After setting your styling is all done, click here in this area and then click "Edit" to add your own awesome content!

If you want to get rid of styling on a bit of text, use the "Remove formatting" button in the editor to strip the text of any formatting and reset your style.

To save the changes you've made, click "Save" on the editor toolbar.

To delete text from the text area, click "Clear".

Insert Images:

Hover the mouse over the image placeholder and click "Select Image".

Select the image either from your computer, or from URL, or from Media Library.

Click "Insert into Post".

Save Template:

The changes you make to the template are saved automatically.

To close the template editor, click "Close editor" at the right side.

Send Test Email:

We highly recommend that you send a test email created from the template to yourself to make sure it displays as it should.

Click "Send test email" at the right side, enter your email address and click "Send" button.
