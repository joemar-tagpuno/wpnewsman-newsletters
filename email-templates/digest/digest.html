How to Create and Edit Digest Templates

To add blog posts to the template:

1. Use the options in the editor to modify the template as you need.
2. Click "Close" at the right side to save the template.

  • Put the mouse at the place where the first post will start.
  • Click on the "Insert Posts" button on the editor.
  • Select the post type, categories, authors, content ("Full post", "Excerpt" or "Fancy excerpt").
  • Click on the posts you want to add to the template to select them. Click on "Day", "Week" or "Month" button if you want to select the posts for the specified time period.

To delete inserted blog posts from the template:

  • Hold Ctrl and hover the mouse over the post excerpt.
  • Click on the "Remove" button.
