How to Create and Edit Digest Templates

To add blog posts to the template:

1. Click on "Add Post" at the right side.
2. Select "Full post", "Excerpt" or "Fancy excerpt" in the "Use Content" menu.

  • "Full post" includes the entire posts into the message.
  • "Excerpt" includes your own excerpt you wrote when publishing the post.
  • "Fancy excerpt" includes an automatically generated excerpt (50 first words by default).

3. Click on the posts you want to add to the message to select them.
4. Click "Insert".

To edit the template title, posts' titles or excerpts:

  1. Hover the mouse over the item you want to edit.
  2. Click "Edit".
  3. Click "Clear" to clear the text.

To insert images into the template:

  1. Hover the mouse over the image placeholder.
  2. Click "Select image".
  3. Select the image either from your computer, from the URL or from the media library.
  4. Click "Insert into post".

To edit the template header, footer or story (excerpts):

Use the settings under the appropriate tabs: Header, Footer, Story.

To edit the template layout:

  1. Click on "Edit post template" at the right side
  2. Make the changes to the template layout.
  3. Click on the "Save" icon.

To edit the post divider:

  1. Click on "Edit post divider" at the right side.
  2. Edit the divider.
  3. Click on the "Save" icon.
